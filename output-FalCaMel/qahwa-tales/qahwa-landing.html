The Origin

Jareen. The Coffee Arabia Almost Forgot.

In the Asir highlands, where the mountains catch the monsoon mist, terraced farms cling to slopes that seem impossible. Here, one of the only coffees grown in Arabia has survived for centuries.

Jareen is not Yemen. It is not Ethiopia. It is Saudi Arabia's own coffee story, written in elevation and patience. Small farms. Hand-picked cherries. Generations of knowledge compressed into each bean.

When the dallah pours here, 2,000 meters of altitude pour with it. The terroir is not a marketing word. It is the literal earth that shapes what you taste.
