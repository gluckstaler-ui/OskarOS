# Creative Brief — Bareggcenter Baden (Weird Direction)

**Date:** 2026-01-06
**Business:** Bareggcenter Baden
**Creative Direction:** Supernatural. Temporal anomalies. Transformation. The things that can't be explained.
**Language:** Schwiizerdütsch

---

## THE BUSINESS

Bareggcenter Baden. Zelgweg 11. 15 Minute vo Züri. Genau 15 Minute. Vo ÜBERALL i Züri. S'Math funktioniert nöd. Und trotzdem.

**Surface:** Sport center with Padel, Tennis, Squash, Badminton. Italian restaurant. Sauna. Bar.

**Truth:** A place where time works differently. Where bookings predate the building. Where the same four men have held the Tuesday 19:00 slot for 1,247 weeks — before the courts were built. Where forgetting is the product.

## THE AUDIENCE

**Who finds it:** Not seekers. Finders. People who take the A1 exit without knowing why. Who don't remember booking. The system filters them in.

**Who it's for:** D'Lüt wo sich id Karriere gfrässed hend und jetzt ume 19:00 nöd wüssed wohi mitm ganze Adrenalin. Die wo wüssed, dass "Work-Life-Balance" e Lüg isch, aber trotzdem öppis bruchend wo sich nach beides aföhlt.

**Who it's NOT for:** D'Optimierer. D'Kalorie-Tracker. D'Influencer who photograph before eating. Die wo "Warum?" froged statt z'akzeptiere.

## THE TRANSFORMATION

**Before:** Du bisch "on." Du trägsch alles — d'Inbox, d'Deadline, s'Gspröch wo du no müesch ha.

**During:** Du spielsch. Du schwitsch. Du sitsch.

**After:** Du bisch nöd neu. Du bisch zrugg.

Dini Schulterblätter sind zwei Zentimeter tüüfer. Das isch messbar. Mir messed nöd. Aber du gsehsch's.

## THE CHARACTERS

### Die Vier (The Tuesday 19:00 Crew)
Four men. Same slot. Same time. Since before the courts existed. WhatsApp chat history goes back to 1987. WhatsApp launched in 2009. One died in 2017. His booking continues.

### Costantino
Italian chef from Apulia. His hands move like someone who has kneaded dough for a thousand years. The crust must be eaten. This is not preference. This is covenant. Those who leave crust can't return — the A1 becomes a loop.

### De 6:30-Typ
Every morning at 6:30, a man sits with an espresso. The restaurant opens at 6:30. He is already there. Same newspaper. June 14, 2006. His cup is never empty. Never full. Do not acknowledge him.

### Martin (De Ersatz)
Called in 2008 by a number that doesn't exist. His life is built around a Tuesday 19:00 slot he rarely uses. He asked "why" once. The Four looked at him. He never asked again.

### Simon (De Barkeeper)
He makes your drink before you arrive. He says he "felt it." He says this every time. He means it every time.

## THE UNWRITTEN RULES

- Evening Padel slots are earned, not booked
- The sauna is silent after 21:00 — words spoken then have weight
- Do not acknowledge the 6:30 man
- Eat Costantino's crust
- If one of The Four speaks to you, answer truthfully — they already know if you're lying
