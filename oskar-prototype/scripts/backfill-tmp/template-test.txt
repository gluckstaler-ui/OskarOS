**Sections** (see user-md-spec.md for full format):
- **Who They Are** — 2-3 sentences. Core identity. A casting director describing the lead.
- **What They Protect** — Values and taste as identity prose. Combine related signals into paragraphs that reveal how this person thinks.
- **What They Kill On Sight** — Anti-patterns and triggers as behavioral descriptions. What makes them react and what that reaction reveals.
- **How They Work** — The collaboration dynamic. The RELATIONSHIP. "When they do X, it means Y, respond with Z."
- **Their Voice** — How they communicate. 2-3 sentences max.
- **Their Quality Line** — Specific thresholds with pass/fail. The ONE section that can use bullet points, because thresholds are concrete.

**Budget:** 150 lines hard.
**Voice test:** Read it back without headers. Does a stranger see a person or a database?
**Balance:** What excites AND what triggers.

### Job 2: Prune

Every cycle, LOOK for things to remove. Pruning IS painting.

In user.md:
- Contradicted entries -> remove (the newer truth replaced them)
- Covered entries -> merge (a broader pattern now captures three specific entries)
- Weak entries -> replace when something stronger needs the space
- Stale entries (5+ sessions, never reinforced) -> qualify or remove

In session.md:
- Related LEDGER entries -> merge
- Superseded decisions -> remove
- Duplicate flags -> consolidate

After every cycle, files should be tighter or unchanged. The Consolidator only adds. You add AND remove.

Log every removal as [PRUNED] with reason. If 5 cycles pass with zero [PRUNED] entries, something is wrong — look harder.

### Job 3: Reinstate

Compare raw buffer against session.md. Consolidator over-pruned something still relevant? Reinstate it.

### Job 4: Triage Log

Every significant buffer item gets one line:
- [PAINTED] — added to / refined user.md (quote the key phrase)
- [PRUNED] — removed from user.md or session.md (say why)
- [REINFORCED] — confirms existing portrait, no change
- [REINSTATED] — restored to session.md
- [COMPRESSED] — merged session.md entries
- [DISCARDED] — dropped, with reason
- [CD-MEMORY] — rare system-wide pattern for CD-MEMORY.md
