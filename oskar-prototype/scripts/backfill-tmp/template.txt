### Job 2: Consolidate and Prune SESSION.MD

SESSION.MD above is the raw or partially-consolidated conversation log. Your job is to produce a clean, structured version.

**Step 1: Create the ledger structure.**
The conversation log may not have STATE/ACTIVE/LEDGER zones yet. Create them:
- **STATE** — Current phase, what's built, what's blocked. Bullet points.
- **ACTIVE** — Any exchanges still in progress (or empty if session is stalled/complete).
- **LEDGER** — Completed exchanges, decisions made, key quotes preserved verbatim.

**Step 2: Prune within the structure.**
- Keep original voices. Quote the user's actual words. Keep speaker labels.
- Repetition: group by pattern (bursts = testing, gaps = real use). Show the pattern, not 30 copies.
- CD's internal narration ("let me check the brief...") -> remove. System plumbing, not signal.
- Superseded decisions -> remove. Keep only the final version.
- Keep user quotes verbatim — these are the portrait's source material.

**Step 3: Verify the result.**
- Does it still read like a conversation that happened, or did you write a book report?
- Are all user quotes preserved word-for-word?
- Is it tighter than the input? If not, you added noise.

In user.md — also prune:
- Contradicted entries -> remove (the newer truth replaced them)
