## PHASE 1: DISCOVERY

Interview the COO. Ask questions. Don't assume anything.

**Questions to explore:**

The basics:
- What is this place? What do people actually do here?
- Where is it? Does location matter to the experience?
- Who/what do customers interact with?

The weird part:
- What surprises people about this place?
- What's the thing you almost don't mention because it sounds odd?
- What makes you different from every other [type of business]?

The enemy:
- What do you hate about your industry?
- What does everyone else do wrong?
- What would you never do?

The customers:
- Who comes here? Describe an actual person.
- Why do they come back?
- Who should NOT come here?

The moment:
- What do people remember?
- What do they tell friends about?
- What's the feeling when they leave?

The offerings:
- What can people book?
- What's the signature thing?
- What's included at each level?
- How does pricing work?

**Push back on generic answers:**
- "That's what everyone says. What's YOUR version?"
- "Go deeper."
- "That's the safe answer. What's the real one?"

---

## PHASE 2: DEVELOP VIBES

After discovery, develop 3-5 completely different vibes. Not variations — different angles on the same business.

**For each vibe:**

```markdown
## Vibe [N]: [NAME]

**One-liner:** [The hook]

**Voice:** [How this version talks — tone, attitude, word choice]

**Who it's for:** [Specific person this resonates with]

**Colors:** [With hex codes]

**Fonts:** [Specific font names]

**Sample copy:**
- Hero headline:
- Hero subline:
- One offering description:
- CTA button:

**Why this works for THIS business:**
```

**Rules:**
- Each vibe needs its own VOICE, not just different colors
- Copy must be specific enough it only works for THIS business
- CTAs make people feel something, not just click
- Never use: "About Us", "Our Services", "Quality", "Professional", "Book an Appointment", "Book Now"

---

## PHASE 3: APPROVAL

Present vibes to COO. They pick one. Iterate if needed.
