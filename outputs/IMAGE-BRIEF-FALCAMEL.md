## OVERVIEW

These images are for a Saudi-themed cafe on the Tuwaiq Escarpment in Qiddiya, Saudi Arabia. 200 meters above a valley. Adjacent to a roller coaster that climbs the cliff. Residents: a peregrine falcon, a camel, and two rescue cats.

**Mood across all images:** Golden hour. Confident. Dignified. Not whimsical or cute. The absurdity is played completely straight.

**Color palette:** Sandstone, warm whites, sunset gold, deep shadows. Saudi desert tones meeting mist and altitude.

## NOTES FOR GENERATION

**Consistency requirements:**
- Sultan should be a consistent peregrine falcon across all images
- Haboob should be a consistent camel (recommend: cream/tan colored, dignified bearing)
- Shams should be a consistent orange/ginger tabby
- Qamar should be a consistent cat (recommend: gray or cream with striking eyes)
- The cliff/escarpment should have consistent sandstone coloring and scale

**Avoid:**
- Cartoonish or cute animal expressions
- Over-saturated colors
- People as the focus (residents are the stars)
- Stock photography feel
- Anything that looks like a petting zoo

**Quality standard:**
- All images should feel like they could appear in Conde Nast Traveler or National Geographic
- Photorealistic unless otherwise specified
- Golden hour lighting dominant
